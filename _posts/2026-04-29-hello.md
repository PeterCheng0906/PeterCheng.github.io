Welcome. This is the first post on this site.

I built this page to have somewhere to keep my work — projects, papers, notes — that isn't a one-page resume and isn't trapped in someone else's platform. The Projects page is the thing I'll update most: it'll grow as I finish data science, ML, and NLP work between Johns Hopkins and Stanford. The CV is the formal version of the same story.

This blog will be the informal version: short writeups when I learn something worth sharing, lessons from projects that didn't make the resume, and the occasional thought about what it's actually like to work at the boundary of computational humanities and applied machine learning.

If you want to talk — about a role, a collaboration, or anything you found interesting on the site — my email is in the sidebar. Always happy to hear from people working on similar things.

Thanks for stopping by.

— Peter
